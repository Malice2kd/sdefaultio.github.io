WHAT IS THE VR CENTER?

THE VR CENTER OFFERS ONE OF THE MOST UNIQUE GAMING EXPERIENCES IN ROANOKE, VA.

Established in 2016 THE VR CENTER was initially just an idea. But now you can schedule blocks of individual playtime or book Birthday Parties,Team Building Exercises, Social Events, etc.

The VR Center isa full functioning virtual reality arcade with all the major platforms including the HTC Vive, Oculus Rift, and Sony PlayStation VR. We have VR egg-shaped pods which includes roller coasters, adventure rides, and first person shooters. Our 10'x10' rooms are dedicated areas where you can immerse yourself into games like SuperHot, Beat Saber, and Job Simulator.

Player packages are available for anyone who wants to try it all. Come by The VR Center and experience virtual reality today or attend our monthly eSports events.
